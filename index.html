He Who Fights With Monsters

He Who Fights With Monsters is a book series that I've enjoyed thouroughly. There are a total of 10 volumes out and there is more to come. You follow a chracter named Jason Asano and experience the adventures he goes on. One day he was sleeping and next thing you know he wakes up in a different world, in a hedge maze, with cannibals trying to eat him. It's an outrageous story with amazing unique chracters and personalities. The world is rich with history and a vivid description of the seetings and environments makes it so that you can almost picture it. One of my favorite all time reads, it kept my attention through all 180 hours of reading them!
